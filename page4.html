SEIKO 6117-6400 World Time
Once I finalized few mod watches I planned to create Rolex Batman type watch with rolex style case, MM300 style dial and hands. I Planned to use this exploded 6117 watch movement from Ebay there. However once i got the watch I couldn't and I ended up to restore this with aftermarked parts.

After polishing the case and assembled everything it turned out to be even cooler than I expected.

  1. MOVEMENT:  Seiko 6117, 17 jewels, 21,600 bph
  2. DIMENSIONS: Diameter 41mm, THICKNESS 13mm, Lug to Lug 45.5mm, Lug width 20mm
  3. WEIGTH: 100g
  4. CRYSTAL: Acrylic, raised, flat
  5. WATER RESISTANCE: 0m
