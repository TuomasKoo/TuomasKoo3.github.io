SEIKO SSA345 
Presage Cocktail "Espresso Martini"
The second Seiko I bought was s stunning grey Cocktail with beautiful dauphine hands.
With handwind, power reserve and see-trough case back I was sold.

The after market bracelet finalizes the outlook and its nice everyday office watch. 

  1. MOVEMENT:  Seiko 4R57
  2. DIMENSIONS: Diameter 40.5mm, THICKNESS 14.5mm, Lug width 22mm
  3. WEIGTH: 200g
  4. CRYSTAL: Hardlex mineral
  5. WATER RESISTANCE: 50m
